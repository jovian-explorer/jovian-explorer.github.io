About Me

Hi! My name is Keshav. I am a Ph.D. student under the supervision of Prof. Abhirup Datta at the Department of Astronomy, Astrophysics and Space Engineering (DAASE), Indian Institute of Technology Indore (IIT Indore). I have been awarded the prestigious Prime Ministers Reseach Fellowship (PMRF) in October 2023 for pursuing my doctoral studies.

While my present work involves studiying the Sun using radio occultation data from the Indian Mars Orbiter Mission, I am interested in planetary and exoplanetary sciences as well. Sharing my knowledge and passion for astronomy brings me delight. My curiosity about the mysteries of the cosmos never ceases, whether we are chatting about the newest discoveries in exoplanet research or just having fun. I also try to devote some time to outreach and writing blogs as well. Embarking on a celestial journey is not just about exploring the unknown; it’s about sharing the awe and wonder with the world. As an advocate for outreach, I am committed to making the cosmos accessible to all. From organizing stargazing events to inspiring future astronomers, my mission is to bridge the gap between the wonders of the universe and the curious minds eager to explore them. Join me in spreading the cosmic joy beyond the academic realm, where the stars become a source of inspiration for everyone.
